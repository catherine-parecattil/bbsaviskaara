About the Event

Treasure Hunt is a fast-paced 3-day competition where teams race against time and each other, solving puzzles, decoding clues, and completing challenges to advance through multiple elimination phases. The event tests logical thinking, teamwork, creativity, and speed through a mix of digital, physical, and mental tasks. Only the quickest and most accurate teams will progress, with the ultimate goal of uncovering the final treasure!
