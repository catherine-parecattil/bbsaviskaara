About the Event

An **Open Mic** is a live event where individuals can take the stage to perform or share their talents in front of an audience. It is typically open to anyone who wants to participate, making it a great platform for aspiring artists, comedians, poets, musicians, and storytellers. Open mic nights are commonly held at cafes, bars, theaters, or community spaces, creating an informal and supportive atmosphere where performers can showcase their work, gain experience, and engage with the audience.
